All about Liverpool FC

Liverpool FC is a professional football club based in Liverpool, England. The club was founded in 1892 and has played at the iconic Anfield stadium since its formation. Liverpool FC is one of the most successful clubs in English football history, having won 19 league titles, 7 FA Cups, 8 League Cups, and 6 European Cups.

Club Information

  • Full Name: Liverpool Football Club
  • Nickname: The Reds
  • Founded: 1892
  • Stadium: Anfield
  • Manager: Jurgen Klopp
  • Owner: Fenway Sports Group

Recent Achievements

  • Premier League Champions: 2019-20
  • UEFA Champions League Winners: 2018-19
  • UEFA Super Cup Winners: 2019
  • FIFA Club World Cup Winners: 2019
